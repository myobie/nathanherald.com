Work

I and my colleagues are building a new, fun way to privately share stuff with the people you care about most called Shareup. Sharing should be easy. We are currently super focused on building a proof-of-concept and we are excited to be able to talk more about Shareup soon.

As mentioned above, I worked on Wunderlist and I moved to Berlin to work with the team. My focus was on improving the synchronization of data between devices and the general experience of using the product across multiple devices and collaborating with others.

Before Berlin, I worked at Livingsocial in Washington DC. I took care of the mobile website for a while, then moved to SF to work on an incubation team.
