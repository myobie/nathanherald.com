Click or tap anywhere on the canvas to drop a wave. Drop a few and watch them interact. The colors show where waves add together (bright) and where they cancel out (dark).

<nh-include src="canvas.html"></nh-include>

The math is simple: each wave is a sine function spreading out from its origin. At every pixel, the waves add up. Positive values push toward one color, negative toward another. When two peaks overlap you get a bright spot. When a peak meets a trough they cancel and you get nothing.

This is the same physics behind noise-canceling headphones, the shimmer on a soap bubble, and the pattern you see when two stones hit a pond at the same time.
